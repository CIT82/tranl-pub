Yggdrasil - A Charitable Cause

At Yggdrasil, we believe in the power of entertainment to inspire positive change in the world. That's why we're thrilled to announce our commitment to giving back through our Charitable Donation Cause! For every subscription to our streaming service, Yggdrasil pledges to donate 10% of the proceeds to various charitable organizations dedicated to causes close to our hearts. From supporting education and healthcare to environmental conservation and social justice, we're dedicated to making a difference where it matters most.
